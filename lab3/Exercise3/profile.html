Son Goku

Goku is a boy with a monkey tail. He practiced martial arts hard and has the power to transcend ordinary people. Originally believed to be born on Earth, Wukong later learned that he was a, a member of an alien militant race, and that he was beyond the power of ordinary people. His birth name was Kakarotto.

As Wukong grew up, he slowly became the strongest warrior on the planet. He vowed to protect the earth and defeat those who wanted to hurt it. Goku is portrayed as a character who is cheerful and close to others, but often imagines himself fighting. He is able to concentrate his temper and use its destructive power and then turn into a powerful energy attack.

The most famous is the move-turtle qigong (かめはめ波) that he learned from his master turtle immortal. It is a kind of blue qigong fired from Wukong's palm. When it hits an object, it will explode.
